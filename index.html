RECOMB'99

THIRD ANNUAL INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON COMPUTATIONAL MOLECULAR BIOLOGY

April 11 - 14, 1999
Lyon, France

Organized by: INRIA (The French National Institute
for Research in Computer Science and Control)

Sponsored by: ACM-SIGACT

Recomb'99 is the third edition of the Annual International Conference on Computational Molecular Biology. The first edition, Recomb'97, was held in Santa Fe, New Mexico (see pictures) and Recomb'98 in New York City (see pictures).

RECOMB'2000 will take place in Tokyo (Japan)
