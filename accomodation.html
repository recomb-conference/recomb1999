Tourisme Verney agency may book your accommodation for 70 FF (per file). It offers a large panel of hotels with different categories of comfort and price. If you are interested, please fill in the booking form (or the same in Word format) and return it directly to the agency (by e-mail, fax or regular mail, indicated at the top of the form) before February 15th, 1999. After this deadline, the agency can not guarantee availability of rooms.

If you wish to stay in the Hôtel de Lyon Métropole, Tourisme Verney agency may handle your reservation or you may book yourself your room. In this case, please contact the Hôtel de Lyon Métropole at (+33) 4 72 10 44 44. Do not forget to mention RECOMB'99 Conference rate (550 FF/night - breakfast included).
