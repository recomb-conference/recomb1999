Tourism

Lyon is a big city in the east-east-south of France, the capital of Rhône-Alpes region. If you want to travel around, you may contact the Tourist Information Center (Office de Tourisme de Lyon)

Main Office: Place Bellecour 69002 Lyon, phone +33 4 72 77 69 69, fax +33 4 78 42 04 32
Saint-Jean: Avenue Adolphe Max 69005 Lyon, phone +33 4 78 42 25 75
Perrache: Railway station 69002 Lyon
Fourvière : Esplanade de Fourvière 69005 Lyon
